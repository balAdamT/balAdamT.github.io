GW Goal Efficiency Calculator

How To Use
This calculator will give you information on what it will take in time and resources to meet your GW goals.
It is only as accurate as your reported average time; use a timer and record yourself from 'start' to 'repeat quest' for best results. You can adjust honors to account for sharing raids with someone else; however token count assumes host and MVP. Do your best! Meat is assumed at 3 per ex, 4 per ex+; If these numbers are wrong please bully my crew captain. Meat calculation is gain rate for ex/ex+ and consumption for NMs. You can leave time blank if you aren't interested.

If you are looking to calculate general time efficiency, use this tool.
